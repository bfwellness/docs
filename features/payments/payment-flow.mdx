# Flusso Pagamenti

Processo di registrazione pagamenti.

## Flow

1. `PaymentCreateWidget` - Insert amount, method, discount
2. `PaymentConfirmWidget` - Confirm
3. Save to Firestore `/payments`
4. Update `clients.paid` and `clients.toPay`

## Payment Methods

- Cash
- Card
- Bank transfer
- Room charge
